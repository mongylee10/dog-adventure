DOG ADVENTURE

In this fun and exciting game, you get to play as a lovable dog who's on a thrilling adventure, riding on an airplane! Your mission is to help your furry friend navigate through the obstacles and challenges that come their way while in the air. Your job is to guide safely through each level by jumping.

HOW TO PLAY:

  1. Press "SPACE" to start the game and it will instanly start playing
  2. Press "SPACE" to jump and avoid obstacles (clouds and buildings)
  3. To restart the game, press "SPACE" again
